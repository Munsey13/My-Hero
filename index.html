Hero's In Uniform

A random number is given at the start of the game. Each uniform below will have a value, once you click on one of the uniforms a number will be added to you're total score. You won't know that value of each uniform until you click on one of them. You win the game by matching you're total score to the random number, you lose if you're total score goes exceeds the random number. Once you win or lose the game will restart and the random number and uniform's will have new values.
