Hero's In Uniform

A random number is given at the start of the game which is the enemy score. Each uniform logo below will have a value, once you click on one of the uniform logos a number will be added to your total score. You won't know the value of each uniform logo until you click on of them. You win the game by matching your total score to the enemy number, you lose if your total score exceeds the random number. Once you win or lose the game will restart and the random number and values for the uniform logos will have new values.
